Welcome to UWI AR Maps

Directions using Overhead Maps & Aumented Reality

Get simple, user friendly and interactive directions with use of Augmented Reality and Overhead Map on the Faculty of Science and Technology at UWI.

Overhead Maps

You can get directions from your current location using our overhead map feature. Find your classes quickly and easily.

Augmented Reality

A virtual reality experience is also availible to find lecture, tutorial rooms, etc. Explore the world around you!

QR-Code

Want confirmation that you are at the correct location and you are at the right class? Use our QR-Code scanner.

Help

Need Help Navigation our application? The Help section of the application provides answers to some FAQ.

App Demo and Documentations

The code for the project is accessible on github. Developers can fork and contribute to the project. The aim is to create a service that business can use to map their property. This can be used by their employees and customers. A demo of the app working and the relevant documentations can be found below.
